Intersting Facts About Cranes

  • Cranes are very powerful flyers. Some even have the ability to fly over the Himalayas.
  • The mating dance of cranes is spectacular
  • During migration cranes fly with their head and neck straight out and their feet and legs straight behind them.

Interesting Facts About Bunnies

  • Bunnies teeth never stop growing!
  • To express happiness, bunnies will sometimes jump around and flick their heads and feet.
  • Like deer, a female rabbit is called a “doe” and a male rabbit is called a “buck.”

Interesting Facts about Camels

  • The eyes of a camel have three eyelids and two rows of eyelashes that prevent sand from entering their eyes.
  • When they find water, they will drink as much as possible. Also, they can drink up to 40 gallons of water at once.
